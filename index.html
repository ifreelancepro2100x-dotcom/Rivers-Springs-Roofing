A Roofing Service for Every Situation Your Property May Face

Sandy Springs homeowners and Fulton County business owners deal with a wide range of roofing demands. Our 22 specialized services cover the full spectrum — from first notice of damage to final warranty registration.

Asphalt Shingle Roof Replacement

Sandy Springs' most popular roofing service. We install complete GAF, Owens Corning, and CertainTeed systems — not just shingles — with manufacturer warranty registration and a signed workmanship guarantee every time.

Storm & Severe Weather Restoration

When a Georgia supercell tears through Fulton County, our response crews mobilize within hours — tarping, securing, and documenting damage with the detail insurance adjusters require for full claim approval.

Hail Damage Inspection & Repair

Hail bruises shingles invisibly but the damage is progressive and covered by most policies. Our calibrated documentation process gives your insurer everything they need to approve your claim without negotiation.

Commercial Roof Systems

Firestone-certified flat and low-slope membrane roofing for Sandy Springs commercial real estate — from independent retailers on Roswell Road to multi-building office campuses near Perimeter Center.

Precision Roof Leak Detection

Ceiling stains and interior water marks rarely appear directly beneath the actual entry point. We use controlled water testing and thermal imaging to pinpoint the true leak source before any repair work begins.

Fascia & Soffit Replacement

Deteriorating wood fascia creates moisture entry points behind your gutters that rot your roof edge from the inside. We replace with sealed, color-matched composite or aluminum that performs for decades.

Metal Roofing Systems

Standing seam steel and corrugated metal roofing systems for homeowners seeking a 50-year roof with superior wind resistance, energy reflectivity, and virtually zero ongoing maintenance demands.

Skylight Repair & Replacement

Aging skylight seals and improper original flashing cause chronic leaks in Sandy Springs homes built in the 1980s and 1990s. We diagnose the actual failure point and resolve it permanently — not temporarily.

Roof Cleaning & Maintenance

Algae, moss, and lichen growth actively degrade shingle granules and accelerate aging. Our biocide soft-wash treatment restores appearance and extends roof life without damaging granule adhesion or warranty coverage.

What 35 Years of Roofing in Sandy Springs Has Taught Us

Longevity in this industry is not accidental. It is earned through four operating principles that we have never compromised — on any project, for any reason.

Spec-Accurate Installation

Every fastener, overlap, and flashing detail follows manufacturer specifications exactly. Deviating from spec — even slightly — voids warranties and creates long-term leak pathways. We do not take those shortcuts.

Proactive Communication

We contact you before problems arise — not after. Schedule changes, material delays, and weather holds are communicated before your window, not after you have been waiting at home all morning.

Responsible Waste Management

Tear-off debris is sorted and disposed of through licensed haulers. We bring correctly-sized dumpsters so debris does not spill onto your driveway, and we sweep nails with calibrated magnets after every project.

Referral-Driven Growth

The majority of our new business comes from existing customers referring neighbors. We never take that trust for granted — every project is treated as an opportunity to earn the next referral from a satisfied homeowner.
